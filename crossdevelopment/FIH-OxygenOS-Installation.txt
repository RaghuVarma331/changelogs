• unlock your bootloader
• enable developer options
• enable usb debugging
• connect phone to computer & open command prompt with adb drivers installed.
• type adb devices 
• type adb reboot bootloader
• type fastboot devices
• resize system and vendor partitions to 3.5G S & 1G V using my repartition tool (https://forum.xda-developers.com/t/guide-repartition-tool-for-nokia-6-1-plus-x6.4334461/)
• reboot to bootloader
• type fastboot devices
• unpack oxygen os zip
• click on install.bat 
• once after you reach oxygen os boot animation logo please wait for 8 to 14 minutes to boot completely.
