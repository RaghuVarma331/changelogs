How to flash ROMs?

Clean flash 
=========

* Boot in to twrp
* Click on wipe
* Click on format data
* type yes
* Reboot to bootloader
* Boot in to twrp 
* Wipe all partitions
* flash ROM zip
* Change slot
* Wipe all again except system vendor
* Reboot system


Dirty flash?
=========

* Boot in to twrp
* Flash rom zip
* Change slot
* Reboot system
